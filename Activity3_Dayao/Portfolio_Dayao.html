Services Offered


🐾 Purr-fect Stay Package

 Treat yourself and your beloved feline to a luxurious staycation! Our Purrfect Stay Package includes cozy accommodations, delicious meals, and plenty of playtime. Rest assured, you and your cat will feel right at home in our safe and comfortable environment.


🐾 Whisker Wellness Check

 Keep your cat in tip-top shape with our Whisker Wellness Check. Our experienced veterinarians will perform a comprehensive health assessment, including vaccinations, parasite control, and dental care. Your cat's health and happiness are our top priorities!


🐾 Meow Massage Therapy

 Pamper your cat with our Meow Massage Therapy session. Let our expert masseuses soothe away tension and stress, leaving your furry friend feeling relaxed and rejuvenated. Treat your cat to the ultimate spa experience!


🐾 Bath-time Bonanza

 Treat your cat to a luxurious bath-time experience with our Bath-time Bonanza service. Our gentle and experienced staff will pamper your furry friend with a soothing bath, using premium cat-friendly products to leave their coat clean, shiny, and refreshed. Say goodbye to tangled fur and hello to a clean and happy cat!


🐾 Whisker Watch Program

 Heading out of town? Leave your cat in good hands with our Whisker Watch Program. Our trained caretakers will provide round-the-clock supervision and care, ensuring your cat receives the attention and affection they deserve while you're away. Enjoy peace of mind knowing your furry friend is in safe hands at Nikuuu's Cat Haven!
